About the Company

Orient Travel is a value based Travel Management Company founded in Vancouver, Canada by long time friends Rafael Amorim & Gabriel Silva. After 15 years in the travel industry our co-founders went into business together in 2015 to look at leisure travel from a different perspective. Growing quickly, a key fundamental to our early success has been our outstanding personal service, commitment to technology, and complete transparency. We will never mislead a customer for our own benefit, and have proven that the travel industry is ripe for disruption from the traditional (that means old) approach. Proudly Canadian we like doing good business and are committed to serving clients on their terms.

Our Mission

How many companies can honestly say that every member of their team understands their mission statement? Very few. At Orient Travel we wanted a concise statement that embodied our purpose, and that all members of our team could live and breathe every day. So here it is in all its glory.
Our mission is to reduce the cost and complexity of travel management through unparalleled personal service, complete transparency, and a continued focus on innovation.
